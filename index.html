What is an International Edition?

An International Edition TextBook is simply the International Counter Part to a United States Edition. Most editions have different covers and different ISBN's on the outside covers. These book were originally created to be sold in different regions. Most International Edition TextBook have identical contents as United States Edition. Occasionally, some subjects such as Management, Accounting, Finance, may have different end chapter case studies & exercises.


Is it legal to buy International Edition TextBooks?

Yes, it is Totally LEGAL to use International Edition TextBooks. The United States supreme court has asserted your right to purchase International Editions, and ruled on this issue. You can find a New York times article about their decision.
